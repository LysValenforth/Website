Hi, I'm Lin.

This website is a small space on the internet where I try to make sense of my thoughts by turning them into words. Here, you'll find a collection of my writing, from dreamy poems to pieces I like to think of as “literature.”

A little digital escape where I can overthink, romanticize everyday things, and sometimes pretend I’ve got it all figured out through writing. Whether it came from a late-night burst of inspiration or a quiet moment, if it meant something to me, it might show up here.
